About DoIHaveADHD.org

DoIHaveADHD.org is a free resource created to help you better understand Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD). Our self-assessment tools and guides are designed for education and reflection — not diagnosis. Whether you're just curious or actively seeking help, we’re here to support your journey toward clarity and care.

Disclaimer: This website does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for personal medical concerns.
